1. Project Overview

"IRON CORE" is a proposed web-based e-commerce platform dedicated exclusively to high-performance gym supplements. The system is designed to provide fitness enthusiasts, bodybuilders, and athletes with a seamless, aggressive, and highly transparent shopping experience for protein powders, creatine, pre-workouts, and vitamins.

2. Problem Statement

Currently, many local supplement stores lack an intuitive, trustworthy online presence. Customers struggle with verifying product authenticity, navigating cluttered interfaces, and experiencing slow checkout processes. IRON CORE aims to solve this by providing a modern, premium, and reliable digital storefront.

3. Key Features & Modules

  • ❯
    Dynamic Product Catalog: Categorized viewing for Protein, Creatine, and Pre-workouts with high-quality imagery and detailed nutritional facts.
  • ❯
    Shopping Cart System: Real-time cart updates without page reloads, calculating totals, taxes, and shipping dynamically.
  • ❯
    User Authentication: Secure login and registration for customers to track order history and save shipping details.
  • ❯
    Admin Dashboard: A backend interface for the store owner to manage inventory, update prices, and view sales analytics.
